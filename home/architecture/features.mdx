## Scheduling

**Path**: `app/calendario/`

Full calendar interface built on FullCalendar.io with:

- Day, week, and month views
- Drag-and-drop booking management
- Team resource views (multi-practitioner)
- Google Calendar sync
- Temporal blocker events
- Real-time availability checking

## Patients

**Path**: `app/pacientes/`

Comprehensive patient management:

- Patient database with search and filtering
- Clinical records and medical data
- Intake forms
- Patient import (bulk) and export (Excel via SheetJS)
- Duplicate detection and merging
- Patient discharge workflows
- Tabs for active, inactive, and deleted patients

## Billing

**Path**: `app/finanzas/`

Financial management:

- Payment tracking and history
- Invoice management
- Financial reports
- WhatsApp payment collection links

## Prescriptions

**Path**: `app/recetas-medicas/`

Digital prescription system:

- Prescription generation with templates
- Medication management
- QR code generation
- MINSAL (Chilean health ministry) compliance

## Team

**Path**: `app/equipo/`

Team operations:

- Member management and onboarding
- Role-based permissions (`lib/permissions.ts`)
- Booking team views
- Seat assignments
- Profile configuration

## Integrations

**Path**: `app/conexiones/`

Third-party service connections:

- **Fonasa**: Chilean public health insurance integration
- **AI transcriber**: Automated session transcription
- **Google Calendar**: Two-way calendar sync
- **Mux**: Video hosting for online sessions (`app/sala-online/`)
