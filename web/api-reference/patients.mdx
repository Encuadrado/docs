## Module location

- **Services**: `encuadrado_web/modules/patients/services/`

## Overview

The patients module manages patient records, contact information, and their relationship to professionals and bookings.

## Key model: `PatientModel`

Stores patient demographics, contact details, and identification (RUT for Chilean patients).
